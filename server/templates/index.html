Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does it take to charge my scooter?

    A full charge takes approximately 45mins - 1 hour

  • Is it cheaper compared to charging at home?

    Our hybrid microgrid charging station utilises solar energy as our primary source of electicity which is much cheaper compared to the conventional grid. In fact our prices at Rs 0.07/kW is almost 50% cheaper than charging from home.

  • Does the charger work if it's raining?

    During bad weather conditions we utilise our backup battery and the grid to provide you with an uninterrupted source of charging you EV.
